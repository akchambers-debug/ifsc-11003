Story Overview

The Princess and the Frog is a Disney animated film released in 2009. It follows Tiana, a hardworking young woman in New Orleans who dreams of opening her own restaurant. After an unexpected encounter with Prince Naveen, she is magically transformed into a frog, leading to a journey of love, perseverance, and self-discovery.

How Tiana Becomes a Frog

  1. Meets Prince Naveen
  2. Kisses him to break the curse
  3. Gets turned into a frog herself
Key Terms
Voodoo
A spiritual practice used by Dr. Facilier to cast curses.
Bayou
A marshy area where much of the frog adventure takes place.
Dream
Tiana’s lifelong goal of owning her own restaurant.
